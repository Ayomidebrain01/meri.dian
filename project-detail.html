The Residence

Penthouse Living
Redefined

The Alderton presents 48 meticulously designed penthouse residences atop a landmark tower on Fifth Avenue. Each home features floor-to-ceiling glazing, private terraces with panoramic Central Park views, and interiors crafted by the David Collins Studio — one of the world's most celebrated luxury design practices.

Rising 62 storeys above the Upper East Side, The Alderton represents the culmination of Meridian's design philosophy: uncompromising architecture that engages its setting while creating homes of lasting beauty and value. Every residence occupies a full floor or a curated half-floor configuration, ensuring privacy and light from every aspect.

Residents enter through a private porte-cochère into a double-height lobby of book-matched Calacatta marble. The building's amenity programme — spanning 28,000 sq ft across four dedicated levels — rivals the finest private members' clubs in the city.
