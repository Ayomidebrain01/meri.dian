01
Vision &
Site Acquisition

Everything begins with a precise understanding of where luxury demand meets scarcity. Our acquisitions team evaluates dozens of opportunities each year, selecting only those with the potential to become landmark addresses. We look beyond zoning and price to assess cultural capital, neighbourhood trajectory, and long-term value creation.

◈
Market Analysis

Comprehensive demand modelling, comparable sales analysis, and buyer profile mapping before any site is considered.

◆
Due Diligence

Legal, environmental, and structural review conducted by our in-house team of specialists.

✦
Feasibility Study

Full financial modelling including construction cost, yield projection, and return sensitivity analysis.

02
Design &
Architecture

We commission the world's finest architects and interior designers — selecting talent whose aesthetic vision aligns with the specific character of each site. Our design process is collaborative, rigorous, and uncompromising. We never value-engineer design decisions that would diminish the integrity of the finished product.

◉
Architect Selection

We work with globally recognised firms including BIG, SOM, and Zaha Hadid Architects, matched to each project's character.

▲
Interior Design

Interiors are treated as seriously as architecture — with separate commissions given to world-class interior studios.

●
Sustainability Integration

LEED and WELL certifications are built into design from day one, not retrofitted at the end.

03
Planning &
Approvals

Navigating complex urban planning environments requires deep expertise and long-standing relationships. Our planning team has successfully guided projects through some of New York's most challenging approval processes — from landmark preservation requirements to complex air rights negotiations.

◈
Permit Management

In-house planning experts manage all municipal permit processes, typically reducing approval timelines by 30%.

◆
Community Engagement

We invest in community consultation at every project, ensuring local stakeholders understand and support our vision.

04
Construction &
Craftsmanship

We build with a network of specialist contractors and artisans developed over two decades. Our construction management team is on-site daily, ensuring that every detail is executed precisely to specification. We apply the same scrutiny to a door handle as we do to a structural system.

◉
Quality Control

Our proprietary 240-point quality checklist is applied at every stage of construction — from foundation to finishing.

▲
100% On-Time Record

Meridian has never missed a committed completion date across 47 projects — a record we protect fiercely.

●
Artisan Network

Relationships with over 80 specialist craftspeople in metalwork, stone, bespoke joinery, and decorative plaster.

05
Sales &
Delivery

Our sales process is as considered as our design process. We don't operate open showrooms or rely on third-party brokers for primary sales. Instead, our dedicated in-house sales team cultivates relationships with qualified buyers and their advisors — ensuring each home reaches the right owner through a curated, private process.

◈
Private Sales Process

Curated introductions to qualified buyers with white-glove consultation from enquiry to completion.

◆
Bespoke Customisation

Buyers at pre-sale stage work with our design team to personalise finishes, materials, and floor plan configurations.

06
Stewardship &
Aftercare

Our commitment to residents does not end at completion. The Meridian Resident Services team is available to every homeowner indefinitely — managing warranties, facilitating introductions to maintenance professionals, and coordinating building services. We believe in the properties we build for the long term.

◉
5-Year Warranty

Every Meridian home comes with our comprehensive 5-year structural and finish warranty — double the industry standard.

▲
Concierge Service

Dedicated resident services team available 7 days a week for all maintenance, service, and lifestyle requests.

Our Standards

The Non-Negotiables

Standard 01
100% On-Time Delivery

We have never missed a committed completion date. This is a cultural commitment enforced from the top and built into every contractor agreement we sign.

Standard 02
LEED Certification on Every Project

All Meridian developments achieve a minimum of LEED Gold certification. Our most recent 12 projects have all achieved LEED Platinum.

Standard 03
No Value Engineering on Design

We never reduce the quality of materials or design elements to improve margins. If the numbers don't work at the quality level required, we don't proceed with the project.

Standard 04
5-Year Full Warranty

Every home we build carries a 5-year comprehensive warranty covering structural integrity, mechanical systems, and all finish elements — double the statutory requirement.
