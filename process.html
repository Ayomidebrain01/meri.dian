Our Methodology

Six Stages to
Exceptional

Each stage is governed by Meridian's non-negotiable standards — design integrity, material excellence, programme certainty, and white-glove client experience.

01
Months 1–3

Vision & Site Acquisition

Every project begins with a question: where can we create something truly extraordinary? Our acquisition team identifies and evaluates dozens of sites per year, applying rigorous financial modelling and contextual analysis before a single penny is committed.

↗ 3–6 months
Neighbourhood and comparable market analysis across target geographies
Financial feasibility modelling and IRR projections
Conceptual massing and density studies
Off-market acquisition strategy to secure best-in-class sites
02
Months 3–10

Design & Architecture

We commission the world's finest architectural and interior design practices — choosing partners who share our obsession with excellence. The design process is iterative and uncompromising; we never accept "good enough."

↗ 6–12 months
Global architect and interior designer selection and briefing
Iterative design development and client presentation
Material and finish specification — no value engineering
Sustainability and LEED/WELL certification target setting
03
Months 8–16

Planning & Approvals

Our in-house planning and legal teams navigate the approval process with expertise built over 23 years in the most complex regulatory environments in the US. We maintain relationships with all key agencies and community stakeholders.

↗ 6–18 months
Full planning and zoning application management
Community engagement and public presentation strategy
Environmental and structural engineering sign-off
Pre-construction sales launch and reservation programme
04
Months 16–36

Construction & Craftsmanship

Meridian self-manages all construction activity, partnering with a curated network of specialist subcontractors who share our standards. Our on-site quality control programme involves weekly inspections against a 400-point checklist.

↗ 18–30 months
In-house construction management and programme oversight
400-point weekly quality inspection protocol
International procurement of bespoke materials and fittings
Regular buyer update programme and site tour events
05
Months 36–40

Sales & Delivery

Our sales process is built around the buyer experience, not volume. We offer bespoke one-to-one consultations, fully furnished show residences, and a personalised onboarding programme that ensures every resident feels truly at home from day one.

↗ 3–6 months
Private preview events and off-market sales to existing clients
Bespoke show residence tours by appointment
Custom finish selections and upgrade packages
White-glove move-in coordination and residence orientation
06
Ongoing

Stewardship & Aftercare

Delivery is the beginning of our most important work. Meridian remains involved in every building we create — overseeing building management, maintaining relationships with residents, and ensuring the building continues to perform to the standard it was built to.

↗ Ongoing
Dedicated building management and 24/7 concierge team
Annual resident satisfaction surveys and improvement programme
Proactive maintenance and capital investment programme
Resale support and secondary market advisory
