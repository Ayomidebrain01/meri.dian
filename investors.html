FAQ

Investor
Questions

What is the minimum investment with Meridian?
+

Minimum investment thresholds vary by structure. Project-level equity co-investments begin at $5M, fund commitments at $10M, and mezzanine debt positions from $3M. We work with a limited number of investors per project to maintain alignment and service quality.

How are investor returns distributed?
+

All structures feature a preferred return to investors before any profit is shared with the Meridian team. Distributions are made quarterly during the investment period, with the bulk of capital and profit returned upon project completion or fund exit.

Is Meridian open to new investor relationships?
+

We selectively add new investors to our platform on a relationship-by-introduction basis. The best first step is to reach out to our Investor Relations team directly. All enquiries are handled with complete discretion.

What reporting do investors receive?
+

Investors receive comprehensive quarterly reports covering project progress, financial performance, market conditions, and upcoming milestones. Annual audited financial statements are provided for all fund vehicles. Our investor portal provides real-time access to all documentation.

How does Meridian manage construction risk?
+

Our 100% on-time delivery record reflects a rigorous risk management framework: fixed-price construction contracts, performance bonds, comprehensive insurance, a dedicated QA team, and a proprietary 240-point quality control checklist applied throughout every project.
