Who We Are

Redefining
Residential Excellence

Meridian Development Group has spent two decades shaping skylines and creating communities that set the benchmark for luxury residential living. Our portfolio spans Manhattan penthouses, Connecticut estates, and Hamptons retreats.

The Meridian
Process

Vision & Site

We identify and acquire sites with exceptional potential, conducting thorough market analysis and architectural feasibility studies to validate every investment thesis.

Collaborating with leading architects and designers, we translate vision into meticulous plans — balancing aesthetic ambition with structural integrity and livability.
