Our Origin

Founded on a
Bold Vision

Meridian was founded in 2001 by CEO Jonathan Hale with a singular conviction: that luxury housing could be both architecturally extraordinary and responsibly built.

Starting with a single brownstone conversion in Manhattan's Upper West Side, Meridian quickly earned a reputation for uncompromising quality and design integrity. Within a decade, the firm had expanded into large-scale residential developments across New York, Connecticut, and New Jersey.

Today, our portfolio spans everything from intimate boutique residences to landmark tower developments — each project unified by our unwavering commitment to place, craft, and lasting value.

We work exclusively in the luxury segment, where our clients expect the extraordinary. We never disappoint.

Jonathan Hale — Founder & CEO, New York 2001

Our Journey

Key
Milestones

2001
Founded in New York

Jonathan Hale founds Meridian with the acquisition of a West 82nd Street brownstone conversion.

2006
First Ground-Up Tower

Completion of The Rowan — a 42-unit luxury tower in Tribeca that sells out in 11 days.

2010
Connecticut Expansion

Meridian enters the suburban luxury market with the acclaimed Westover Estate community in Greenwich.

2014
First LEED Platinum Project

The Solent achieves LEED Platinum certification — the first luxury residential development in New York State to do so.

2017
$1B Portfolio Milestone

Meridian's total completed portfolio value exceeds $1 billion across 28 projects.

2019
Hamptons Market Entry

Shore Reserve becomes our first Hamptons development — setting new records for waterfront luxury pricing.

2022
NAHB Developer of the Year

Meridian wins the National Association of Home Builders' highest honor for the first of back-to-back years.

2024
The Alderton Launch

Our most ambitious project to date — 48 penthouse residences on the Upper East Side — launches to international acclaim.
